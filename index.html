    Awards & Papers

    • Jenq-Haur Wang and Ting-Wei Yeh, "Unsupervised Opinion Targets Expansion and Modification Relation Identification for Microblog Sentiment Analysis," Accepted and to appear in Proceedings of the 5th International Conference on Social Informatics (SocInfo 2013), Kyoto, Japan, Nov. 25-27, 2013.

  • 2012/09/02: Congratulations! Our paper has been accepted by AIRS 2012.
    • Han-Chih Liu and Jenq-Haur Wang, "Aggregating Opinions on Hot Topics from Microblog Responses," Accepted and to appear in Proceedings of the Eighth Asia Information Retrieval Societies Conference (AIRS 2012), Tianjin, China, Dec. 17-19, 2012.

  • Information Security: system and network security, content protection: digital rights management
